About me

My name is Jordon Ogonoski. I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Alberta. I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Anthropology. I have been interested in coding from a young age.

My first experiences with coding was when I enrolled in a robotics class in high school. I enjoyed it so much, and it became my passion which lead to completing the remaining courses in high school. While I did help build the robot, my passion was in coding the autonomous functions. In grade 12 I competed in a provincial competition and won first place with my team and alliance. I then went on to compete in the 2015 Vex Robotics World Championship.

Being able to compete in an international competition is one of my greatest accomplishments. This achievement further pushed me to persue a life in coding and to increase my knowledge in the field.

MyHighSchool App

Towards the end of my time in high school, my younger brother Evan came to me with an idea to develop an app for our school. He wanted to develop it for Apple devices and asked for my help to build it on Android devices. I then spent the next three years going from knowing nothing about building phone applications, to starting an app developing company with my brother and a friend. We designed and built the MyHighSchool app, the perfect social media platform for students in high school, their teachers, and their parents. While we have had difficulties in brining it to the market, we are still hopeful that one day we can get it into the hands of students.
